About

I am a PhD candidate in Hospitality & Tourism Management at Virginia Tech, where I work with Dr. Juan Luis Nicolau. Before starting my PhD, I spent seven years at Samsung Electronics in B2B tech marketing and also worked as a licensed tourist guide in Korea. My research focuses on understanding tourism and hospitality using quantitative methods. I apply econometrics and data analytics to examine how consumer behavior and market dynamics shape industry outcomes.
