I also study tourism economics and examine how strategy, finance, and technological innovation shape industry outcomes. ## Research Interests

Consumer behavior in tourism and hospitality; Tourism economics; Strategic management and finance; Econometrics and big data analytics; Technology, innovation, and artificial intelligence.
