Research

My research examines consumer behavior and behavioral economics in tourism and hospitality, particularly how individuals make decisions under different market conditions. I also study tourism economics and examine how strategy, finance, and technological innovation shape industry outcomes. My work combines econometrics and data analytics to explore these questions.

Research Interests#

Consumer behavior in tourism and hospitality; Tourism economics; Strategic management and finance; Econometrics and big data analytics; Technology, innovation, and artificial intelligence.
